SEO and SEM

Search engine optimisation is all that stands between you and the terrifying silence of the internet. If your content isn’t optimised nobody will find it. If nobody finds it...sad music, tumbleweed.

Your search engine optimisation strategy needs to increase natural, organic or earned results for Google, Bing, Yahoo and the rest. We use white hat SEO to focus content on users and make it easily accessible to spider algorithms.

When organic search boosts just aren’t enough we can provide clear search engine marketing tactics to help you pay your way to the top.

Search engine optimisation

Technical optimisation, authority optimisation and content optimisation: each of these are critical components of a solid SEO strategy.

Our SEO services will increase your visibility within algorithmic, organic search results to deliver high quality, targeted traffic to your website. None of our SEO work comes out of the box - every service is tailored to your services and products to boost your plans and hit your targets.

We will carry out keyword and market research and complete a technical SEO audit to figure out the best way to boost your rankings. We’ll tell how you best to optimise your content, how to boost your chances of obtaining backlinks and how your sitemap should be configured to make Google, Bing and the others sit up and take notice.

Search engine marketing

There’s a lot of competition out there, which means search engine optimisation by itself is often not enough. That’s where the darker art of search engine marketing comes into play.

SEM involves the promotion of your sites by increasing their visibility in search engine results pages, known as SERPS. SEM includes SEO tactics, combined with paid search advertising, such as pay-per-click listings and social media marketing.

People use search engines to find a solution to a problem, an answer to their question or to learn how to do something. Our targeted SEM campaigns will make sure it’s you they come to to get those questions answered.

Social media focus

It’s clear by now that social media is here to stay. People find things online increasingly through social media marketing campaigns and recommendations from friends via social media, so we’ll make sure your site and services are optimised for Facebook, Twitter, Instagram and whatever the boffins come up with next.

Secnix’s social media linking plus strategic, targeted SEO and SEM equals online powerhouse. You’re welcome.
