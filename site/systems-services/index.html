RESILIENT SYSTEMS

SYSTEMS SERVICES

Hyper-convergence, containerised, orchestrated infrastructure, diverse appliances, continuous integration & deployment, cloud native, distributed systems, replicated storage, automation, maintenance, proactive monitoring, backup and recovery.

Infrastructure

With the fundamentals of Open Systems Interconnect, we use RedHat Enterprise Linux, OpenShift Container Platform, Kubernetes on metal or in-cloud implementations such as OpenStack, AWS, and Azure. All containers meet Open Container Initiative image specifications.

LEARN MORE

Distributed Systems

Fault tolerant and resilient, our systems use a range of algorithmic, extensible, concurrent, heterogeneous and robust technologies, with status updates at your fingertips. We have the know-how to prevent future failures and scalability unknowns.

LEARN MORE

Maintenance

Maintenance provision and planning for both greenfield projects and inherited systems. We design and build infrastructure support systems that ensure peace of mind, efficient maintenance of ephemeral data, monitoring, backup and recovery.

LEARN MORE

Automation

Automation using the newest technologies including Ansible, YAML, Kubernetes, Python and JSON. We'll look after your automation with simple, safe and straight-forward deployment or a more complex, model-driven process, tailored to your project.

LEARN MORE

Cloud Native

Microservices and containers adhering to the Open Container Initiative in the cloud using OpenShift and Kubernetes across AWS, Azure, OpenStack and more. Data mirrored at multiple sites, and autoscaling giving you only what you need when you need it.
